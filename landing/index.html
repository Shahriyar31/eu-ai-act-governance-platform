// capabilities

Everything compliance
teams actually need

Six integrated modules — each maps to a real EU AI Act obligation or GRC framework requirement.

⚖️
EU AI Act Risk Classification
Rule-engine + LLM hybrid classifier maps any AI system to Unacceptable, High, Limited, or Minimal risk. Pulls live rules from PostgreSQL — no hardcoded logic.
Annex III · Art. 6
🛡️
Automated DPIA Generation
Generates Data Protection Impact Assessments on demand. Identifies data subjects, processing risks, and mitigation measures aligned with GDPR Art. 35.
GDPR Art. 35
🔒
OWASP LLM Top 10 Assessment
Assesses AI systems against the OWASP LLM Top 10 security risks including prompt injection, training data poisoning, and model denial of service.
OWASP LLM 2025
🗺️
NIST AI RMF Mapping
Maps assessment results to NIST AI Risk Management Framework functions: Govern, Map, Measure, Manage. Produces structured remediation guidance.
NIST AI 100-1
🤖
RAG Compliance Assistant
LLM assistant grounded in 665+ EU AI Act knowledge chunks stored in pgvector. Answers compliance questions with citations. Groq llama-3.3-70b with Gemini fallback.
RAG · pgvector · Groq
📡
Regulatory Change Monitoring
Monitors EUR-Lex and EU AI Office for regulatory updates. Detects changes via SHA-256 content hashing, auto-updates the knowledge base, emails admins.
Firecrawl · Resend
